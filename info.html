Our mission

The aims of the Cambridge Popup Speakeasy are to promote jazz as a cultural asset within the Cambridge community. We provide opportunities for musicians to play publicly, to network, to socialise, and to grow as musicians and performers.

In the past couple of years we are delighted to have built a strong following - we are extremely grateful for your support.

The evening is free to attend, and always will be. We hope you enjoy yourself!
