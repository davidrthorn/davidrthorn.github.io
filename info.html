Our mission

The aim of the Cambridge Pop-up Speakeasy is to promote jazz as a cultural asset within the Cambridge community. We provide opportunities for musicians to play publicly, to network, to socialise, and to grow as musicians and performers.

Over the past few years we are delighted to have built a strong following – we are extremely grateful for your support.

The evening is free to attend, and always will be. We hope you enjoy yourself!

Want to perform?

We are constantly seeking new acts, whether individuals or established groups looking for an opportunity to play. We suggest you first attend as an audience member to get an idea of the format of the evening.

All sets are pre-arranged; this is not a jam session. We love diversity and any style of jazz is fine – vocal, cool, latin, modern, fusion, afro-beat, New Orleans, Arabic, free – you name it, we've probably done it!

The most important things are to entertain the audience and enjoy yourself.
