Play by Ear
Ear training for musicians

Play by Ear is an ear training app that helps musicians learn to recognise intervals, chords, and scales by ear. It runs entirely on your device with no account required.

Features

  • Interval recognition exercises
  • Chord identification training
  • Scale recognition practice
  • Customisable difficulty and presets
  • Works offline — optional cloud sync to back up your progress

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 17 February 2026

Play by Ear is an ear training app for musicians. Your privacy is important to us. The app works fully offline by default, and an optional cloud sync feature is available if you choose to sign in.

Data we collect

Play by Ear does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or login is required to use the app.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services are used.
  • No advertising services are used.

Local storage

Your settings, bookmarks, and saved presets are stored locally on your device using standard app storage. Without cloud sync enabled, this data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Cloud sync (optional)

You may optionally sign in with a Google account or Apple account to enable cloud sync. This backs up your settings, bookmarks, and presets so they can be restored if you reinstall the app or switch devices.

When you sign in:

  • We store only your anonymous user identifier provided by Google or Apple. We do not store your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.
  • Your app data (settings, bookmarks, and presets) is stored in Google Cloud Storage, keyed to your anonymous identifier.
  • No personal information from your Google or Apple account is sent to or retained by our servers.

Account deletion

If you have signed in and wish to delete your account, you can do so at any time from within the app. Deleting your account will:

  • Permanently delete all of your data from our cloud storage.
  • Revoke the authentication tokens associated with your Google or Apple account.
  • Sign you out of the app.

Data stored locally on your device is not affected by account deletion and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

In-app purchases

Play by Ear offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock additional features. This transaction is processed entirely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information. These transactions are governed by Google's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy respectively.

Third-party services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The third-party services involved are:

  • Google Play / Apple App Store — for in-app purchases.
  • Google Sign-In / Sign in with Apple — for optional account authentication (cloud sync). Only the anonymous user identifier is used; no personal profile information is accessed or stored.
  • Google Cloud Storage — for storing cloud sync data.

Children's privacy

Play by Ear is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the developer contact email listed on the Google Play store listing.
