Popup Speakeasy is open to all amateur (and sometimes professional) jazz musicians. We encourage everyone to first come along in person to meet the organisers and get a feel for the standard expected.

If you're just getting into playing jazz, the Speakeasy is an amazing place to meet jazz musicians, seek advice and start the journey to getting on stage. There's always tons of talent in the room and everyone is approachable and friendly!
